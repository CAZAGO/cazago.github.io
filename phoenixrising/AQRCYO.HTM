PHOENIX RISING:
COMPETITION FOR YOUNG ORGANISTS


The AGO/Quimby Regional Competition for Young Organists will be held on Saturday, June 20, 2009. The event will be held at Chaparral Christian Church in Scottsdale. The competition instrument is a 52-rank 3-manual Casavant Frères.

Competitors in this event will be comprised of the winners of Region IX chapter competitions.

Awards: Regional: $1,000 cash prize to first-place winner; $500 cash prize to second-place winner.

The winner will also receive paid registration and expenses for the convention, and will be presented in a recital at Phoenix Rising: Region IX Festival 2009. All first-place winners of this competition will be invited to perform at the 2010 National Convention in Washington, DC.

Deadlines for chapters wishing to hold a local competition, as well as repertoire requirements, official rules, and competitor applications, were published in the April 2008 issue of The American Organist. The information is also available on the AGO website.
