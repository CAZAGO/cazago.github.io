Executive Committee Members (Members-at-Large)


Executive Committee Members are elected for a 3-year term, 1 member per year from ballot


3 members total: 1/ 3 elected each year


1. Attend all meetings of Executive Committee.


2. Serve as advisors to Executive Committee.


3. Serve as committee chairs.


4. Assist other officers and committee chairmen.


5. Serve as liaison between general membership and Executive Committee members.
