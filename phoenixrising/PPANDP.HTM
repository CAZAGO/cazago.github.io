PHOENIX RISING:
PEDALS, PIPES AND PIZZA


A partial-day event that introduces children and youth to the pipe organ. It will be led by Dr. Bonnie Loney, Dr. Mark Ramsey, and assisted by AGO Region IX Education Coordinator, Dr. Linda S. Margetts. Participants will have the opportunity to play the pipe organ and will learn about its design and history. The event will culminate with a private lunchtime party and closing concert by Theater Organist, Lew Williams, at Organ Stop Pizza.

The Central Arizona Chapter of the American Guild of Organists offers several scholarships for the purpose of helping new and continuing students with organ study. Piano students of junior high and high school age who live in Central Arizona may apply for an organ scholarship. Use this link for detailed information and a scholarship application form.
