The only existing hotel in the world with a Frank Lloyd Wright-influenced design, the Arizona Biltmore has been an Arizona landmark since its opening on February 23, 1929 when it was crowned The Jewel of the Desert. The resort's design was "inspired" by consulting architect Frank Lloyd Wright, who collaborated with former student Albert Chase McArthur.
