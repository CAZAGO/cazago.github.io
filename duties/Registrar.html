Registrar

1. Attend all Executive Committee meetings

2. Keep up to date records of current membership

3. Forward any membership data changes to person maintaining database.

4. Forward to Membership committee the names of those attending

5. Send new member names and addresses to Pipeline editor for publication. Also send members' changes of addresses and telephone numbers to Pipeline editor.

6. Keep a supply of membership brochures and have them available at meetings, recitals, and workshops (reading sessions, church organizations, schools and universities, etc.)

7. Call members whose Dues are delinquent.

8. Route member interests to appropriate chair persons.

9. Prepare a report of annual activities to be presented orally at last Board meeting of the year and the written copy to be filed by Historian.
