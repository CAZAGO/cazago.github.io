PIPELINE EDITOR

1. Be responsible for editing and basic formatting of submitted articles.

2. Collect and compile articles and information from Chapter Officers, Coordinators, and members. Submit as a single galley document (digital file) to Pipeline design and layout person.

3. CONTENT: Information in the newsletter is based upon certain basic monthly inclusions.

A. A list of Officers and their phone numbers.

B. Upcoming Guild meetings.

C. Calendar of upcoming local organ recitals: Guild–sponsored, Guild member‘s recitals, or other music and worship events.

D. Column from the Dean and Sub–Dean.

E. Open church position notices.

F. News of special projects within the chapter.

G. Yearbook Updates

H. Editor has the authority to determine content with the understanding that he/she works in consultation with the Board. Practical considerations include space limitations, timeliness of material and priorities set by the Board.

I. Each edition will include the official disclaimer: “Articles, features, or advertisements in THE PIPELINE do not necessarily represent the views and/or endorsements of the Central Arizona Chapter AGO. Articles may be edited to accommodate space and formatting requirements.”
