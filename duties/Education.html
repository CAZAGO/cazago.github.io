Education Coordinator


1. Attend all executive Committee meetings


2. PROMOTE the expansion of professional skills and knowledge among the Chapter membership through

A. Service planning/playing

B. Wedding planning/playing

C. Repertoire

D. Hymnal materials

E. Presentation of Guild Exam program and related materials


3. CREATE a resource pool (e. g. tapes, articles, study guides) from the AGO Educational Resources list and other sources, which shall be available to the Chapter membership


4. Motivate members to make the personal effort to take the Guild Exams


5. Sponsor Guild Exam preparation.


6. Develop educational programs or activities in the community which will promote interest and appreciation of the organ and its music. Possible formats include:

A. Pipe Organ Encounters

B. Special concerts for young people or senior citizens

C. Organ crawls

D. Organ encounters for school children

E. Organ Scholarships for Teenage Pianists


7. Prepare a report of annual activities to be presented orally at the last Board meeting of the year and the written copy to be filed by the Historian.
